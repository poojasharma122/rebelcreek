Is parking available at all racecourses?
Yes. Every Rebel Creek venue offers free on-site parking. Some festivals or meets may offer reserved or accessible spots for guests with specific needs.
Do you offer shuttle services?
Shuttles operate for select major meets and festivals. Check the relevant racecourse page or event preview closer to the date for details.
Are the racecourses accessible for guests with disabilities?
Yes. We provide accessible parking, ramps, viewing platforms, and washrooms at all venues. Please reach out ahead of your visit if you need assistance or priority access.

What time should I arrive?
Gates generally open around two hours before the first race. We recommend arriving early to explore the grounds, view the paddock, and grab a race program.
Can I bring my own food or drinks?
Policies vary slightly by venue. In general, small snacks and sealed water bottles are allowed in general admission areas. Full food vendors are available on-site.
Is betting available at Rebel Creek tracks?
Yes. Each venue offers staffed betting kiosks and mobile betting options. Our staff are happy to help first-time bettors get started.
Can I bring a dog or pet?
Only certified service animals are permitted in most public areas. Some festival-style events may offer designated pet-friendly zones, which will be noted on the event page.
