Race courses

Rebel Creek Race courses

Explore Canada's Premier Horse Racing Venues

Rebel Creek is proud to operate a national network of racecourses, each offering a distinct and unforgettable experience for fans of Canadian horse racing. From Ontario to the Yukon, our tracks deliver world-class racing, breathtaking settings, and authentic regional culture. Whether you're a lifelong racing enthusiast, an owner or trainer, or a first-time visitor, Rebel Creek offers something for everyone.

Designed with racing excellence in mind, each venue features a unique track layout, high-quality racing surfaces, and modern facilities. From turf to dirt, boutique venues to flagship tracks, Rebel Creek represents the future of Canadian horse racing. Browse our full list of racecourses below and discover why race day at Rebel Creek is unlike any other.

Rebel Creek Downs

📍 Ontario

Rebel Creek Downs is the cornerstone of our racing network and a flagship destination for thoroughbred racing in Ontario. The expansive 1.4-mile turf track, with its wide bends and extended home straight, is built for drama, stamina, and bold finishes. It attracts some of the most competitive fields in the country, drawing top-tier jockeys, trainers, and horses year-round.

This venue plays host to The Northern Cup, Rebel Creek’s headline event, and is known for its electric atmosphere on race day. Beyond the track, Rebel Creek Downs offers exceptional amenities — from luxury corporate boxes to vibrant family zones — ensuring a premium experience for every guest. The seamless blend of heritage and innovation makes this venue a must-visit for Canadian racing fans.

Amenities

  • 🏇 Turf track (1.4 miles)

  • 🏟️ Grandstand with full course views

  • 🏢 Hospitality suites and corporate boxes

  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family enclosure with shaded seating

  • 🍔 Food courts and licensed bars

  • 🛝 Children's play area

  • 🅿️ Free parking

  • ♿ Accessible seating and facilities

  • 💵 Betting windows and ATMs

  • 🎶 Live music on major race days

Rebel Creek West

📍 Alberta

Rebel Creek West brings the raw power and speed of dirt racing to life in the heart of Alberta. This track is famed for its tight corners and fast, hard surface, creating edge-of-your-seat sprint racing that rewards brave tactics and skilled riding. It's the go-to track for thrilling finishes and action-packed evenings.

The venue truly shines at night. Floodlights cast a glow over the dust as the Sunset Stakes takes centre stage, combining racing with live entertainment, DJs, and gourmet food trucks. Whether you’re in the rooftop bar or standing rail-side with a cold drink, Rebel Creek West delivers a high-energy, unforgettable experience for every racegoer.

Amenities

  • 🏇 Dirt track (1 mile)

  • 💡 Floodlit course for night racing

  • 🌇 Rooftop terrace with views

  • 🎤 Live music stage

  • 🚚 Food trucks and mobile vendors

  • ♿ Wheelchair-accessible facilities

  • 🅿️ Ample on-site parking

  • 🎰 Betting kiosks and mobile integration

  • 🚌 Shuttle from downtown Calgary

Rebel Creek Central

📍 Manitoba

Located in the heart of the prairies, Rebel Creek Central is a traditional flat racing venue that champions heritage and community. With its mile-long oval turf track and generous layout, it’s a favourite among jockeys for its fairness and rhythm. Race fans are drawn to the intimate viewing experience and laid-back, welcoming atmosphere.

The Prairie Mile is the course’s standout event, offering a rich purse and a proud regional following. Designed with accessibility and comfort in mind, Rebel Creek Central is perfect for families, long-time punters, and those looking for an authentic race day that puts the horses at the centre of it all.

Amenities

  • 🏇 Flat turf track (1 mile)

  • 🪑 Covered tiered seating

  • 🍖 Picnic areas and BBQ pits

  • 👀 Paddock viewing zone

  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Family-friendly pricing

  • 🧸 Kids' activity zones

  • 🏷️ Community discounts

  • 🅿️ Free parking

  • 💵 ATMs and betting windows

Rebel Creek North

📍 Manitoba

Rebel Creek North embodies the spirit of rural racing. Set amidst rolling prairie fields, this seasonal racecourse comes alive during the warmer months with colourful crowds, community pride, and the beloved Harvest Cup Festival.

Though smaller in scale, it punches well above its weight in atmosphere and hospitality. The shorter oval turf track makes for fast-paced, up-close racing, while the festival-style layout, artisan markets, and relaxed vibe make it a true gem in Canada’s racing calendar.

Amenities

  • 🏇 Short turf oval

  • 🪑 Covered bleachers

  • 🌭 Festival food vendors

  • 🎪 VIP paddock tent

  • 🐾 Pet-friendly zones

  • 🅿️ Free field parking

  • 🚻 Accessible portable toilets

  • 🎰 Betting kiosks

  • 🛍️ Artisan market stalls

Rebel Creek East

📍 Quebec

Just a short drive from Montreal, Rebel Creek East is where racing meets refinement. With a hybrid turf and synthetic track, it offers one of the most technically demanding layouts in the country. The venue is popular with both Canadian and northeastern U.S. stables, adding a rich international flavour to race day.

Bilingual service, boutique hospitality, and the glamorous St. Laurent Classic bring together the elegance of European race culture with the intensity of North American competition. Whether you’re there for the racing or the experience, Rebel Creek East delivers both in style.

Amenities

  • 🏇 Turf/synthetic hybrid track

  • 🗣️ Bilingual guest services

  • 🥪 Bistro-style food vendors

  • 🍾 Champagne tent

  • 🌆 VIP skyline lounge

  • 📺 Betting lounge with live data

  • 🚆 Parking and train access from Montreal

  • 🎨 Art displays and fan zones

Rebel Creek Valley

📍 British Columbia

Set within the wine-rich interior of British Columbia, Rebel Creek Valley offers a boutique race day experience in a vineyard setting. This compact turf track is designed for fast, tight racing and delivers the perfect mix of sport and serenity.

The signature event, Vintner’s Dash Weekend, blends exciting thoroughbred action with curated wine tastings, acoustic music, and local artisan stalls. With sunset views and vineyard picnic areas, this is Canadian racing at its most relaxed and refined.

Amenities

  • 🏇 Compact turf track (7 furlongs)

  • 🍇 Vineyard-style picnic spots

  • 🌅 Sunset viewing decks

  • 🍷 Winery food pairings

  • 🎸 Acoustic stage

  • 🚌 Hotel shuttles

  • 🎟️ Lawn and general admission access

Rebel Creek Ridge

📍 Nova Scotia

Overlooking the Atlantic, Rebel Creek Ridge offers a uniquely coastal racing experience in Nova Scotia. The natural elevation and sea breezes add a dynamic challenge to the turf track, creating unpredictable and thrilling race outcomes.

It’s a venue steeped in local flavour, with maritime food vendors, folk music, and a loyal community of fans. The Atlantic Sprint Series is the course’s annual highlight, showcasing the region’s best horses in front of a spirited, seaside crowd.

Amenities

  • 🏇 Natural turf track with elevation

  • 🪑 Bleacher and hillside seating

  • 🦞 Seafood vendors and local fare

  • 🍾 Ocean-view VIP lounge

  • 🎵 Maritime live music

  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Family admission specials

  • 🚐 Limited RV parking

  • 🎰 Community betting facilities

Rebel Creek Frontier

📍 Yukon

Rebel Creek Frontier stands alone — quite literally — as Canada’s northernmost racecourse. Located in the Yukon, it delivers a rugged, unforgettable race day experience with its half-mile dirt track and wide-open northern skies.

Operating only in the short summer season, the venue’s Northern Lights Stakes is a fan-favourite, often run under the surreal glow of dusk. This course channels the pioneering spirit of old-school racing with modern hospitality touches. Expect bonfires, music, community connection — and horses ready to race, no matter the latitude.

Amenities

  • 🏇 Half-mile dirt track

  • 🪑 Timber bleachers

  • 🔥 Bonfire circle & acoustic acts

  • 🍔 Camp-style concessions

  • 🐴 Horse meet-and-greet areas

  • 🅿️ Field parking

  • 🤠 Western-themed family zones

  • 🔋 Solar-powered betting terminals
