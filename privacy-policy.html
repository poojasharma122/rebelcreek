Privacy Policy

Rebel Creek is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website: rebelcreek.ca.

By using our website, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name and email address (if you contact us or use our contact form)

  • Anonymous usage data (e.g. IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent)

  • Technical data through cookies (see Section 5)

We do not collect payment information or any sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to enquiries sent through our contact form

  • Improve the performance and user experience of our website

  • Monitor site traffic and usage patterns (via tools like Google Analytics)

  • Send email updates (only if you voluntarily opt in)

We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party advertisers.

3. Who Has Access to Your Information

Personal information may be accessed by Rebel Creek administrators for the purposes of communication, support, or internal reporting. We do not share your personal information with third parties unless:

  • It is required by law

  • You have given explicit consent

  • It is necessary to provide a service you have requested

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal data against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access. All communication with the site is encrypted via SSL (HTTPS). Data submitted through our forms may be stored securely on third-party platforms (e.g. web hosts, email services) that meet industry standards for data protection.

5. Cookies and Analytics

Rebel Creek uses cookies and analytics tools to understand website traffic and improve performance. Cookies are small data files stored on your browser.

We use:

  • Google Analytics: to track general website traffic

  • Functional cookies: to remember preferences or form entries

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites, such as individual racecourses, media outlets, or tourism resources. Rebel Creek is not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies if you visit their sites.

7. Your Rights

Under Canadian privacy law (PIPEDA), you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you

  • Request correction of any inaccurate data

  • Withdraw consent or request deletion of your data at any time

To make a request, contact us at info@rebelcreek.ca.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. All changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We recommend checking this page periodically to stay informed.
