Personal Data Policy

Rebel Creek is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with Canada’s Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). This policy outlines how we collect, store, use, and safeguard personal data submitted through rebelcreek.ca.

1. What Is Personal Data?

"Personal data" refers to any information that can identify you as an individual. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your name

  • Your email address

  • Your IP address (when visiting our site)

  • Any information you voluntarily provide via our contact forms or email

2. When Do We Collect Personal Data?

We may collect personal data when:

  • You submit a contact form

  • You email us directly

  • You interact with our website (IP address, browser data, etc.)

We do not collect personal data without your knowledge or consent.

3. Why We Collect Personal Data

We collect personal data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to enquiries or requests

  • To provide information or support related to Rebel Creek venues or events

  • To improve website functionality and user experience

  • To send updates, if you’ve opted into communications

We do not use personal data for advertising, resale, or profiling.

4. How We Store & Protect Your Data

Your personal data is:

  • Stored on secure servers managed by our hosting provider

  • Accessible only to authorised Rebel Creek administrators

  • Protected using standard encryption (SSL), access controls, and secure storage practices

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected.

5. Third-Party Access

We may use trusted third-party services (e.g. website hosting, email platforms) to manage your data. These services are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and comply with applicable privacy laws.

We do not share, sell, or rent personal data to any other third parties.

6. Your Rights Under PIPEDA

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you

  • Correct inaccurate data

  • Withdraw your consent for data use

  • Request deletion of your data

To make a data access or removal request, email us at info@rebelcreek.ca. We aim to respond to all requests within 30 days.

7. Data Retention Policy

We only retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill its original purpose or to meet legal or operational requirements. Unused or expired data will be securely deleted.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Personal Data Policy occasionally to reflect changes in legal requirements or our data handling practices. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised date.

9. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about your personal data and how we manage it, please contact:

📧 info@rebelcreek.ca
